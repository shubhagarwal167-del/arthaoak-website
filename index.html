THE STORY

A Vision Carved
in Oak.

ArthaOak didn’t begin with me - it began years ago with my father, who built a business on one simple belief: trust lasts longer than any product.

Growing up, I learned that people don’t just buy furniture; they build their lives around it. At 21, I chose to return home and carry that legacy forward, blending timeless values with a new vision for modern Indian homes.

“My father’s journey taught me how to earn trust. Mine is to ensure we never lose it.”

- SHUBH AGARWAL, FOUNDER
